Chairman's Message

At ASHWAKA MINES, we believe that responsible mining is more than a business - it's a commitment to the planet, to the people, and to the future.

For over two decades, we have built a company rooted in integrity, sustainability, and excellence. Our journey has been one of continuous growth, marked by strong relationships, technological innovation, and respect for the communities and environments in which we operate.

As we continue to harness the mineral wealth of Africa, we remain steadfast in our vision of creating long-term value while upholding the highest ethical and environmental standards.
